Our Story

From precision engineering to pure nutrition — how Geochem Foods Express is transforming Nigerian food production.

From Precision Engineering to Pure Nutrition

Geochem Foods Express grew from Geochem Associates' legacy of precision and excellence. For over a decade, we've applied the same scientific rigor, hygiene standards, and commitment to quality to every pack of Nigerian food we produce.

Based in Nkofia Village, Awka-Etiti, Anambra State, we source locally, process with care, and deliver nationwide — bringing authentic Nigerian flavors to your table with confidence. Every product is tested in our precision lab to ensure it meets international hygiene and quality standards.
