From Precision Engineering to Pure Nutrition

Geochem Foods Express grew from Geochem Associates' legacy of precision and excellence. Today, we apply the same scientific rigor, hygiene standards, and commitment to quality to every pack of Nigerian food we produce.

Based in Nkofia Village, Awka-Etiti, Anambra State, we source locally, process with care, and deliver nationwide — bringing authentic Nigerian flavors to your table with confidence.
